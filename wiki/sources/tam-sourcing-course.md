## Key Concepts Introduced

- **TAM vs ICP** — TAM for strategic sizing, ICP for pipeline targeting. Related but distinct.
- **Pain-first ICP** — Start with pain, not demographics. Who hurts, how often, how badly?
- **Three ICP dimensions** — Firmographic (table stakes), technographic (sophistication), behavioral (timing). The magic is the intersection.
- **Systems thinking vs workflow thinking** — One-time list pulls vs persistent engines that compound. The strategic shift from task to asset.
- **CPJ dataset** — Clay's proprietary 40M+ company, 900M+ contact dataset with real-time updates from 150+ sources.
- **Tiered account routing** — T1 (high-touch, custom research), T2 (medium-touch, templated), T3 (automated, volume). Match effort to opportunity value.
- **Problem Severity x Frequency matrix** — Prioritize ICP segments by how much and how often the pain occurs.
- **Multiple ICP variants** — One company can have 3-6 distinct ICP profiles, each with different targeting, messaging, and sales motions.
- **ICP validation checkpoints** — Market size, competitive landscape, addressable reality. Validate before committing resources.
- **Reverse engineering from closed-won** — The most reliable ICP signal is analyzing what your best customers actually share.
