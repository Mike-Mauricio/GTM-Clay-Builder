# Clay AI ICP Search

Clay's AI-powered ICP generation feature that inverts the traditional list-building process. Instead of manually defining filters (industry, headcount, location), you input your company domain and AI analyzes your website to determine who you sell to — then generates a target company list automatically.

## How It Works

1. Input your company domain (e.g., `clay.com`)
2. AI reads your website — product pages, use cases, customer references
3. AI determines your likely ICP: target industries, company sizes, buyer titles
4. Returns a matched company list with suggested buyer titles (e.g., "Head of RevOps," "BDR Manager")
5. You can click into the search block to refine: adjust industries, geographies, funding stages

## Use Cases

### Primary: Zero-to-List
Start with nothing but your domain → get a qualified prospect list in minutes. Ideal for:
- New SDRs who don't know the ICP yet
- Companies entering new markets
- Quick list generation for event targeting

### Secondary: ICP Sanity Check
Already have a defined ICP? Run AI ICP Search anyway to see if AI identifies segments you're missing. Useful for:
- Validating existing targeting assumptions
- Discovering adjacent verticals
- Finding buyer titles you hadn't considered

## AI ICP Search vs. Manual Find Companies

| Dimension | AI ICP Search | [[clay-find-companies]] |
|-----------|---------------|----------------------|
| Input | Your domain | Manual filters |
| Logic | AI infers your ICP | You define the ICP |
| Speed | Fastest — one input | Requires filter configuration |
| Control | Less — AI decides targeting | Full — you set every filter |
| Best for | Starting from zero, discovery | Known ICP, precise targeting |
