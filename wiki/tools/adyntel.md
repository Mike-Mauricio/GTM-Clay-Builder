# Adyntel

Ad intelligence tool integrated with Clay that reveals competitors' active advertising campaigns. Input any company domain and see up to 10 active ads with creative formats, copy, headlines, and CTAs.

## Capabilities

- View up to 10 active ads per company domain
- See ad creative formats, copy, headlines, and CTAs
- Filter Google ads by media type (text, image, video)
- View campaign start and end dates
- Analyze competitor messaging, positioning, and campaign strategies

## Use Cases

| Use Case | How |
|----------|-----|
| **Competitive intelligence** | See exactly what competitors are saying in their ads |
| **Messaging inspiration** | Study competitor positioning to differentiate |
| **Growth signal detection** | Companies increasing ad spend = growth/momentum signal |
| **ABM targeting** | Identify companies actively investing in advertising |

## In the GTM Stack

Combine Adyntel competitive intelligence with spend analysis to identify companies actively investing in advertising — a [[clay-signals|growth signal]] that often indicates market momentum or new initiatives.
