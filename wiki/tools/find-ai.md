# Find AI

[UseFind.ai](http://usefind.ai/) is an AI-powered search engine integrated into Clay that finds people and companies using natural language queries. Instead of building complex filter stacks, describe your target in plain English and Find AI scans millions of profiles to return matching results.

## How It Works

1. In a workbook, click `+ Add` and search for `Find AI`
2. Type a natural language query (e.g., "Engineering managers at technology companies who work on search infrastructure")
3. Set maximum number of results
4. Choose match type:
   - **Exact** — results meet all criteria (tighter, higher quality)
   - **Partial** — results meet some criteria (broader, more volume)
5. Run the search — matching leads populate in your table

## Pricing

**1 data credit per result.** You're billed only for results returned — if you request 100 matches and only 90 are found, you pay for 90.

## Example Queries

- "Heads of Marketing at SaaS startups"
- "CFOs at manufacturing companies"
- "Angels in B2B SaaS"
- "CIOs in small family offices"
- "AI startups in NYC"

## When to Use Find AI vs. Native Search

| Use Case | Find AI | [[clay-find-people]] / [[clay-find-companies]] |
|----------|---------|----------------------------------------------|
| **Query style** | Natural language descriptions | Structured filters (industry, headcount, location) |
| **Best for** | Nuanced or hard-to-filter ICPs | Standard firmographic/demographic filtering |
| **Match flexibility** | Exact or partial matching | Filter-based (all criteria must match) |
| **Cost** | 1 credit per result | Varies by provider in the waterfall |

Find AI is strongest when your ICP is hard to express through standard filters — niche roles, specific technology stacks, unusual industry intersections.
