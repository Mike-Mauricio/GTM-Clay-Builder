## ABM and Ad Intelligence in Clay

## 🕵️ Competitive Ad Intelligence

Clay integrates with ad intelligence tools like **Adyntel** to show you exactly what your competitors are doing. Input any company domain and see up to 10 of their active ads, complete with creative formats, ad copy, headlines, and CTAs.

For Google ads, you can filter by media type and see their text, image, and video campaigns with start and end dates. This gives you instant visibility into competitor messaging, positioning, and campaign strategies.

You can combine this competitive intelligence with spend analysis to identify which companies are actively investing in advertising and experiencing growth—signals that often indicate market momentum or new initiatives.

## 🎬 End-to-End Campaign Example

Here's how this works in practice. Let's say you're selling marketing automation software and want to run an ABM campaign targeting growing SaaS companies.

**Step 1: Identify Engaged Prospects from Social Interactions**

Use Clay's social listening to track people who are reacting to, commenting on, or engaging with relevant content—your posts, competitor posts, or industry thought leaders.

**Step 2: Enrich Contact Data and Validate Fit**

For each person engaging with your content, enrich their profile to confirm they're decision-makers at your target accounts. Filter out anyone outside your ICP before spending ad dollars.

**Step 3: Enrich for Ad Matching**

If you're uploading to Facebook or Google Ads, use **Versium** to get email hashes. Use conditional formulas to filter out non-ICP contacts and ensure clean data for maximum match rates.

**Step 4: Push to Ad Platforms**

In Clay, you can natively create a custom Meta audience by clicking `Create ad audience` in Exports and connecting your Meta Ad Account. Choose Contact List then map the fields accordingly.

Processing takes around 48 hours and Clay shows the match rate after the sync completes, or the percentage of uploaded company or contact information successfully matched to a Meta account.

**Step 5: Track Engagement**

See which specific people from which target accounts are clicking, viewing, and engaging with your ads.

**Step 6: Trigger Automated Follow-Up**

Add engaged prospects to personalized email sequences, alert sales reps about high-intent accounts, and create retargeting audiences based on their behavior.

#### 🔁 Retargeting with Precision

This approach works powerfully for retargeting campaigns. When someone visits your website, Clay can identify them, enrich their data, add them to a retargeting audience, and push that audience to Facebook. You can serve personalized ads based on which pages they visited and track exactly who converts.
