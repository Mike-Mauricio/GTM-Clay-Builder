## Deep Dive: SendSpark AI Video Generation

## 📹 Harnessing Sendspark and Clay for Personalized Video Outreach

Welcome to our deep dive on Sendspark for Video outreach! Video outreach is quickly becoming a cornerstone of effective digital communication, especially in sales and customer engagement. In this guide, we'll dive into using Sendspark's video integration with Clay, a powerful tool to not just reach out to customers but resonate with them on a personal level. Let’s unveil the process of leveraging these innovative platforms to create natural and engaging videos that can boost your outreach efforts.

### 🔌 Setting Up Your Sendspark Integration

To blast off with your video campaign, you'll need to connect your Sendspark account with Clay.

Connecting Sendspark to Clay:

- Head to Sendspark and pick up your API credentials, because we're going on a coding treasure hunt. Don't forget to create a new key if you haven’t yet!
- Copy your shiny new 'secret key' and API key.
- Head back to Clay, and paste them into your account connection to connect!

### 📹 Crafting Your Personalized Videos

With the groundwork laid, it's time for the exciting part—making those personalized videos!  
  
It’s really quite simple from here:

- Ensure all fields are populated for optimal personalization—name, email, company, background URL. It's the DNA of your message!
- Hit 'Run' and watch as your messages are transformed into video stars
- Be patient as the videos cook up; good things take time.
- Once your videos are ready, you'll get a “success message” in each of the rows!
- Extract the jewel of this process—the direct video link.

You've reached the summit, the peak, the climax—the videos are ready!

- Share your personalized videos with your prospects, celebrate your success, and make sure to track the stellar impact they have on your outreach.
