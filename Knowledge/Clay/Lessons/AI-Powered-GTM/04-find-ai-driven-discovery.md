## Find: AI-Driven Lead Discovery

We've covered the FETC ("fetch") framework and how to maximize your Clay credits. In this lesson, we'll dive into the first step in the FETC framework: **Find**.

This is where every good workflow begins. You can't enrich, transform, or create anything if you don't have the right data to start with.

## 🌟 The Evolution of "Find"

In the original FETE framework, "Find" referred to pulling data from your CRM, your prospecting tools, or Clay's built-in search features.

That's still the foundation...

But with AI, you're not just filtering for basic attributes. You're discovering leads based on complex patterns and inferred signals.

## 🚀 AI-Powered Finding

With AI integration, the "Find" step becomes significantly more powerful. In Clay, you can now:

- Use AI to **analyze a customer's website** and auto-generate a company search based on their ICP
- Run an **AI job title classifier** to find the right personas across your list
- Use Claygent to **discover prospects who meet nuanced, intent-based criteria** (like "founders who've recently spoken about scaling product teams")
- Use AI to **rank and score** the results of a search based on fit,not just firmographics

This level of targeting would be impossible—or painfully manual—without AI. And it's just the beginning.

## 🗓️ What's Coming Next

In the upcoming lessons, we'll show you how to:

- **Find your ICP using AI**
- Uncover **company lookalikes** to discover your next best customer

These powerful techniques will help you identify prospects with unprecedented precision, allowing you to focus your efforts on the accounts most likely to convert.

## 💥 Why "Find" Matters

The quality of your initial data set determines everything that follows in your workflow. When you find the right prospects from the start:

- Your enrichment becomes more targeted and effective
- Your transformations produce more valuable insights
- Your created content resonates more deeply with prospects

By leveraging AI in the "Find" step, you're not just starting with better data—you're building a foundation for significantly better results throughout your entire GTM process.
